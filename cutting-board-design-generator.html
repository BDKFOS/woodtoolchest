How this generator helps

Sometimes the hardest part of a woodworking project is simply deciding what to build. This cutting board design generator gives you a fast starting point you can actually use in the shop.

Click the button to get a random combination of wood species, board style, size, and a couple of optional details. You can use the result exactly as-is, or tweak it into something that fits your tools, scraps, or customer requests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this generator for edge grain or end grain boards?
It can suggest either. Some outputs are simple edge grain layouts, while others lean into end grain patterns.

Can I use this for scrap wood ideas?
Yes. It works well as a quick idea generator when you have a few good species on hand and want a direction.

Does this replace actual build planning?
No. It is a starting point. You should still adjust dimensions, species, and details based on movement, glue-up strategy, food safety, and tool limitations.
