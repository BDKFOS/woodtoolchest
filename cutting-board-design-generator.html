How this generator works

This tool uses the wood species, lengths, widths, thicknesses, and quantities you enter to suggest a cutting board that fits your stock more realistically. It looks at your shortest usable length, your total available strip width, and your material thickness to recommend a board style and approximate finished dimensions.

It is not meant to replace a full cut list or exact yield calculation. Instead, it gives you a strong starting point that makes more sense than a totally random board idea.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this calculate exact yield?
No. It gives a helpful planning suggestion based on your input, but you should still account for milling, saw kerf, snipe, cleanup cuts, and glue-up waste.

Can this suggest end grain boards?
Yes, but it will lean more toward end grain when your thickness and stock mix make that idea more realistic.

What if I only have one species?
The generator can still give you a good single-species board idea with dimensions and a suitable pattern direction.
