      How it works

      What this generator is trying to do

      This version is built around visual quality and practical depth. It creates premium-looking boards with cleaner wood rhythm, realistic grain texture, 3D illusion options, and enough build information to help you decide whether the idea is worth taking to the shop.

      Cleaner visuals

      Wood grain, shadows, bevels, seams, and believable strip layouts make the board feel less like a flat color swatch.

      More depth

      Each result includes difficulty, buildability, sellability, glue-up notes, tool notes, and rough cut direction.

      3D patterns

      Tumbling blocks, woven illusion patterns, parquet layouts, and end-grain grids give the tool a real flagship feature.
